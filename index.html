Whether you are talking in a team meeting or presenting in front of an audience, you have to speak in public from time to time.

You can do this well or you can do this badly, and the outcome will strongly affect the way people think about you. This is why public speaking causes so much anxiety and concern.

The good news is that, with thorough preparation and practice, you can overcome your nervousness and perform exceptionally well.

Toastmasters International District 97P can help you to become the speaker and communicator you would like to be. We welcome you to our Toastmasters showcase on 4th March 2018 (Sunday) from 1:00 pm to 5:00 pm at Tawana Hotel, 80 Surawongse Road, Si Phraya, Bangrak, Bangkok 10500.
This workshop style program is an internationally recognized course designed to develop your public speaking skills and so much more. Learn how to write an impactful speech and use your voice and gestures to create lasting impressions.

Gain confidence in all aspects of communication, including prepared and spontaneous speeches, body language, active listening and critical evaluation. Be empowered with the skills and take control of any environment in which you have been asked to speak and wow your audience.

The Program will be conducted by experienced Toastmasters strictly according to the guidelines provided by Toastmasters International Successful participants will receive an internationally recognized Certificate issued by Toastmasters International District 97P.
